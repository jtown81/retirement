## 1. Overview

Federal Retirement Planner ("App", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

**Our Core Principle**: Your data is yours. All calculations run on your device. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal information.

---

## 2. What Data We Collect

### Data We DO NOT Collect
- Your name or personal identity
- Your salary or financial information
- Your career history details
- Your location
- Your phone contacts
- Your calendar data
- Your browsing history
- Analytics or usage tracking
- Device identifiers (IDFA, AAID)

### Data We DO Collect (Optional)
**If you sign in with Firebase Authentication:**
- Email address (optional sign-in)
- Authentication token for session management
- Basic profile display name

**Purpose**: Enable premium feature access and sync across devices (if you sign in)

**If you purchase Premium (via RevenueCat):**
- Subscription status (active/expired)
- Subscription tier (basic/premium)
- Purchase transaction ID

**Purpose**: Enable premium features and prevent fraudulent access

**If you opt-in to Google AdMob (Basic tier):**
- Advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android)
- App usage patterns for ad targeting
- Approximate location (country/region level)

**Purpose**: Serve targeted advertisements to support free tier development

---

## 3. Data Processing & Storage

### Where Your Data Lives
- **Retirement Plan Data**: Stored locally on your device only (localStorage)
- **Your Device**: Never synced to servers
- **Account Data**: Encrypted by Firebase (if you sign in)
- **Subscription Data**: Managed by RevenueCat (secure servers)

### Data Retention
- **Plan Data**: Stored until you delete the app or clear app cache
- **Account Data**: Retained while your account is active
- **Subscription Data**: Retained while subscription is active
- **Ad Data**: Retained per Google AdMob policy (typically 90 days)

### Data Deletion
You can delete all data by:
1. Opening the app
2. Settings → Clear All Data (if available)
3. Or: Uninstall app → iOS/Android system will delete localStorage

## 6. Your Privacy Rights

### You Own Your Data
- Your retirement plan data is yours
- You have full control over what data you enter
- You can view all data you've provided
- You can export your data at any time

### Access Your Data
To access data we hold:
1. If signed in: Download account data via Firebase Console
2. Contact us with your email address
3. We'll provide all associated data within 30 days

### Correct Your Data
- Edit all data directly in the app
- Contact us to correct account information
- Changes apply immediately

### Delete Your Data
- Delete from app settings
- Or uninstall the app (iOS/Android deletes localStorage)
- Or request permanent deletion via email
- We'll delete account data within 30 days of verification

### Opt-Out of Advertising
- Purchase Premium tier (no ads)
- Or adjust device-level ad settings:
  - **iOS**: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking
  - **Android**: Settings → Google → Manage Your Google Account → Data & Privacy → Disable Personalized Ads

### Withdraw Consent
- You may withdraw consent for Firebase sign-in by signing out
- You may withdraw consent for RevenueCat by canceling subscription
- You may withdraw consent for AdMob by purchasing Premium

## 7. Data Security

### How We Protect Your Data

**In Transit**:
- All communication with Firebase/RevenueCat uses TLS 1.2+ encryption
- Your data is encrypted in transit

**At Rest**:
- Plan data stored locally is protected by device OS encryption
- Account data encrypted by Firebase
- Subscription data encrypted by RevenueCat

**Access Control**:
- Only authorized Firebase/RevenueCat services access account data
- No human access to your personal information
- No sharing with third parties except as noted

### What We DON'T Do
- We don't sell your data
- We don't share your data with marketers
- We don't use your data for non-service purposes
- We don't have a backdoor to access your local data

### Security Breach
In case of a security breach:
1. We'll notify affected users within 7 days
2. We'll describe what data was affected
3. We'll explain steps to protect yourself
4. We'll describe measures taken to prevent recurrence
