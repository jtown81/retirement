# Federal Retirement Planning Simulation

A local-only retirement planning tool for U.S. federal employees under FERS.

## What This App Does

- Models your federal career (pay, grade/step progression, locality)
- Tracks and projects annual and sick leave balances
- Projects TSP (Traditional & Roth) balances over time
- Calculates your FERS eligibility date and basic annuity
- Models post-retirement expenses including the expense smile curve
- Compares retirement scenarios side-by-side
